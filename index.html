Vikri Aulia

Vikri Aulia is a dedicated Lecturer in the Information Technology Education program at Universitas Negeri Padang (UNP), Indonesia. With a passion for education and technology, he brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to his role, inspiring and mentoring students to excel in the dynamic field of information technology. In addition to his teaching responsibilities, Vikri is a highly experienced Network Administrator with a proven track record of success in managing and securing complex networks. He is committed to staying abreast of the latest advancements in network technology and best practices, ensuring that UNP remains at the forefront of digital innovation.

Introduction to Coding using C

Introduction to Coding using C is a foundational course designed to provide students with a comprehensive introduction to programming concepts and practices. Tailored for non-programmers, this course offers a gentle yet thorough exploration of coding principles using the C programming language. Through hands-on exercises and practical examples, students will develop a solid understanding of fundamental coding concepts, including variables, loops, functions, and control structures. Emphasizing simplicity and clarity, the course utilizes straightforward examples to ensure ease of comprehension and application. By the end of the course, students will have acquired essential coding skills and the confidence to tackle more advanced programming challenges.
